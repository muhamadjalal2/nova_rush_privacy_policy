Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 6, 2026

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Nova Rush ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and shares information about you when you use our mobile game application ("the App"). By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Account information (display name, email) if you sign in with Google
  • Game progress, scores, achievements, and settings
  • Communications with other players via guild chat

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Device model, operating system version, and device identifiers
  • App usage data and gameplay statistics
  • Crash reports and performance data
  • Advertising ID (for ad personalization)
  • Firebase Cloud Messaging tokens (for push notifications)

2.3 Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services that may collect information:

  • Firebase Authentication — account creation and sign-in
  • Cloud Firestore — online data storage and sync
  • Firebase Analytics — usage analytics
  • Firebase Crashlytics — crash reporting
  • Firebase Remote Config — app configuration
  • Firebase Cloud Messaging — push notifications
  • Google Mobile Ads (AdMob) — advertisements
    AdMob may collect advertising ID, IP address, device information, and location data to serve personalized ads.
  • Google Play Games Services — leaderboards and achievements
  • Google Sign-In — authentication
  • In-App Review — app store rating prompts

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide, maintain, and improve the App
  • To sync game progress across devices
  • To display leaderboards and social features
  • To serve advertisements (including personalized ads where consent is given)
  • To send push notifications (with your permission)
  • To analyze usage and fix crashes
  • To comply with legal obligations

4. Advertising and GDPR Consent

The App uses Google Mobile Ads (AdMob) to display advertisements. We use the Google User Messaging Platform (UMP) to obtain your consent for personalized advertising in compliance with GDPR (Europe) and similar privacy regulations.

You can manage your privacy choices at any time from the App's Settings screen under "Manage Privacy Choices". Resetting your choices will re-prompt the consent dialog.

5. Data Storage and Security

  • Local data is stored on your device using SharedPreferences
  • Online data is stored on Firebase servers (Google Cloud Platform)
  • We implement reasonable security measures to protect your data
  • Firebase services encrypt data in transit and at rest

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data:

  • With third-party service providers listed in Section 2.3
  • To comply with legal obligations
  • To protect our rights and safety

7. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:

  • Access, update, or delete your personal data
  • Withdraw consent for data processing
  • Opt out of personalized advertising
  • Request data portability
  • Lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority

To exercise these rights, contact us at the email below.

8. Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 (or 16 in the EU). If we learn that we have collected personal information from a child without parental consent, we will delete it.

9. Data Retention

We retain your data as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the service. You may request deletion of your data by contacting us. Analytics and crash data may be retained in aggregated, anonymized form.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top.
